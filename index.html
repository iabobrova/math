About Me

I am a researcher in mathematics, working on noncommutative geometry, integrable systems, and the Toda hierarchy.

Research Interests

  • Noncommutative generalizations of the Toda hierarchy
  • Integrable systems and Lax pair structures
  • Hirota bilinear equations and discrete hierarchies
  • Painlevé equations and monodromy theory

Selected Publications

  • Bobrova, I., Monodromy structures in Painlevé VI, Ann. of Math. Studies, 2023.
