About Me

I am a researcher in mathematics, working on noncommutative integrable systems and related Poisson structures. I obtained my PhDs at HSE University (Moscow, Russia) and URCA (Reims, France) in 2023, and now I am a postdoc at Max-Planck-Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (Leipzig, Germany).

Research Interests

  • Painlevé equations and monodromy theory
  • Integrable systems and their non-commutative generalisations
  • Poisson structures and cluster algebras
